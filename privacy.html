Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 25, 2026  |  Last Updated: April 25, 2026

NorthVale LLC (DBA NorthVale Properties) ("NorthVale Properties," "we," "us," or "our") operates this website (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you visit our Site or submit information to us.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Full name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Property address (street, city, state, ZIP)
  • Any additional details you provide in our forms

We may also automatically collect non-personal information such as browser type, IP address, pages viewed, and time spent on the Site.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Contact you regarding your property inquiry via phone, email, and SMS text messages
  • Provide you with a cash offer for your property
  • Respond to your questions and requests
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Improve the functionality and performance of our Site

3. SMS / Text Message Communications

By submitting our web form and checking the SMS consent box, you expressly consent to receive automated and manually dialed text messages from NorthVale Properties at the phone number you provide. These messages may include follow-up about your property inquiry, offer updates, and general communication related to your request.

  • Frequency: Message frequency varies based on your inquiry status.
  • Opt-Out: Reply STOP to any text message to unsubscribe at any time. After opting out, you will receive one final confirmation message.
  • Help: Reply HELP to any message for assistance, or contact us at northvaleproperties@gmail.com.
  • Message & Data Rates: Standard message and data rates may apply depending on your wireless carrier plan.
  • Consent Not Required: Consent to receive SMS messages is not a condition of any purchase or service.

4. We Do Not Sell Your Information

NorthVale Properties does not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes. We may share your information only with service providers who assist us in operating our business (e.g., CRM platforms), and only under confidentiality agreements.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by law. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us at northvaleproperties@gmail.com.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at northvaleproperties@gmail.com.

7. Cookies

Our Site may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve user experience. You can control cookie settings through your browser.

8. Third-Party Links

Our Site may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Site is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the Site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:
